Understanding Urban Noise Pollution

Urban noise is a complex challenge with overlapping sound sources. Vehicular traffic accounts for 65% of ambient urban noise, while industrial activities and construction contribute to noise events exceeding 100 decibels.

Health Consequences

Cardiovascular Health

Cognitive Performance

Noise above 65 decibels increases cognitive decline risk by 40%.

Sleep Disruption
